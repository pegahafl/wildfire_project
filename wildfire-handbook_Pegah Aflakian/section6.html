Stay Low to the Ground
Get as close to the ground as possible to avoid superheated air, which rises.
Find Natural Firebreaks
Seek shelter behind large rocks or in an area that has already burned.
Protect Yourself from Smoke
Cover your nose and mouth with a wet cloth to reduce smoke inhalation.
Keep Your Clothes Dry
Wet clothing will scald under high heat. Use natural fibers that resist ignition.
